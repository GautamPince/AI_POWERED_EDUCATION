# MENTOR HIRING & QA FRAMEWORK

**The Trust Backbone**

## 1. Mentor Role Definition
- **IS:** Accountability guide, Performance interpreter, Motivation stabilizer.
- **IS NOT:** Teacher, Influencer, Content creator.

## 2. Ideal Mentor Profile
- **Mandatory:** Cleared same/higher exam OR 2+ years guiding. Strong vernacular comms.
- **Red Flags:** "Motivational speaker" vibe, only YouTube experience, wants to teach concepts.

## 3. Hiring Funnel
1.  **Screening:** Resume + Exam Proof + Vernacular writing sample.
2.  **Case Simulation:** "Student score dropped after 10 days study. What do you do?"
3.  **Live Mock (20 min):** Review fake dashboard, explain next steps.
4.  **Trial (Paid):** 2 weeks, 5-10 students.

## 4. Mentor Playbook (SOP)
- Weekly call structure.
- Allowed vs Forbidden advice.
- Escalation rules.
- Study plan modification limits (No improvisation).

## 6. Feedback Loop
- Post-interaction 1-click rating.
- Anonymous feedback option.

## 7. Escalation
- Missed Session -> Warning.
- Repeated -> Suspension.
- Integrity Issue -> Immediate Removal.

## 8. Capacity & Incentives
- **Cap:** 40-50 students/mentor.
- **Pay:** Base + Retention Bonus + Outcome Bonus. **No rev-share.**
